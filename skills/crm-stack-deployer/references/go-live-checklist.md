# Go-live checklist

- Inbound WhatsApp message appears in Chatwoot inbox.
- Bot responds and can transfer to human queue.
- Confirmed order creates private note in target conversation.
- Contact attributes update (`ltv`, `last_order_*`) after order sync.
- Follow-up worker sends messages and marks `recompra_agendada=true`.
- Nginx and certbot renew flow works without manual intervention.
